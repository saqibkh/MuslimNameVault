Khaliloollah

Boy

Meaning

Friend of Allah (Title of Ibrahim)

Origin

Arabic

The name Khaliloollah is a classic masculine title rooted in Arabic tradition. It carries a divine connection, translating essentially to 'Friend of Allah (Title of Ibrahim)'. This bestows a sense of spiritual protection upon the bearer.

In Arabic culture, names are more than labels; they are prayers. Khaliloollah is widely respected for its positive connotations. It is an excellent choice for a modern Muslim family honoring tradition.
